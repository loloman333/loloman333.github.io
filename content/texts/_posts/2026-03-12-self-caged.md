---
title: self-caged
date: 2026-03-12
tags: Poetry English
---

a roof protects <br>
a wall supports <br>
a shelter hides <br>
a house comforts <br>
a prison, in your mind <br>

burn it down <br>
wreck it all <br>

the sky guides <br>
the wind blows <br>
the far shows <br>
the nature pulls <br>
freedom, takes it all <br>
